How to join

Dues are $15 owed to the club president at the first practice of the season or at the time of committing to the club.

Tournament registration takes place at the beginning of the season. Our governing body is USA Racquetball.

Equipment can be provided for new players.

Practice is held at Fetzer Hall

To get to the courts from the Fetzer Hall entrance walk straight to the back hall around the basketball courts. Continue past the rock climbing area to the end of the hallway. Go up the stairs on the left to the second floor. From the SRC entrance proceed down the hallway to the right past the check in table. Go up the stairs to the second floor.
